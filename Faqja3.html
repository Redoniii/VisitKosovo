The National Library of Kosovo is the largest library in the country. Located in the capital city of Prishtina, it serves as a cultural and educational center, providing access to books, magazines, and other resources to the people of Kosovo. The library has a rich collection of over 500,000 items, including rare books and manuscripts, and it is an important resource for researchers, students, and others who are interested in Kosovo's history and cultural heritage.

Discover the Beauty of Prishtina

Discover the Beauty of Prishtina is a slogan that invites you to explore the capital city of Kosovo. Prishtina is a vibrant and dynamic city, with a rich cultural heritage, charming architecture, and a thriving arts and food scene. From its bustling markets and street vendors, to its traditional teahouses and modern cafes, Prishtina is a city full of surprises and wonders waiting to be explored. Whether you're interested in history, culture, or just soaking up the local atmosphere, Prishtina has something for everyone, and is sure to leave a lasting impression on all who visit.
