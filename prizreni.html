The Prizren Hammam

The Prizren Hammam is a traditional bathhouse in the city of Prizren, Kosovo. It is a cultural and historical landmark and attracts tourists for its traditional Ottoman-style architecture and atmosphere. The bathhouse is a popular destination for locals and visitors seeking to relax and experience the traditional culture of the region.

The Prizren Fortress

The Prizren Fortress, also known as Kalaja, is a medieval fortress located in the city of Prizren, Kosovo. It is situated on a hill overlooking the city and was built in the 11th century during the Byzantine era. The fortress was later expanded and reinforced by the Ottoman Turks in the 16th and 17th centuries. It has served as a military stronghold, as well as a symbol of power and prestige for various empires throughout history.

The Prizren League

The Prizren League was a political organization established in 1878 in the city of Prizren, Kosovo. The league aimed to unite Albanians and protect their rights and interests within the Ottoman Empire. It is considered to be the first national political organization of Albanians and played a significant role in the Albanian national awakening movement. The Prizren League and its congresses are seen as important milestones in the history of Albanian nationalism.
